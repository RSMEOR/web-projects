Importance of weaponry

  • Protection
  • Target practice
  • Deterrents

Guns are important tools that can be the difference of life and death in a violent situation.

Whether you are a gun enthusiast or not, it is hard to doubt the beauty of firearms.

From bulky and massive to sleek and portable, they come in all shapes and sizes.

A weapons style isn't solely for aesthetic purposes, most times the weapons curves, grooves and shape is aimed at improving user experience and functionality.

Like a vehicle, its shape can be admired for its design and at the same time its specific purpose.

Although when related to as a tool, it becomes clear that besides for how a weapon might look, or your personal political opinion on them; this day and age it is undeniable it is a vital tool to ensure a certain level of security and safety, whether at your home, or in the public sphere.
